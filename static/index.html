About Optimus

Incorporated under the Companies Act and registered with the National Council for Construction (NCC Grade 5B), Optimus Building & Civil Contractors Ltd combines local ownership with global standards. Clear communication, collaborative planning, and uncompromising safety are the cornerstones of every engagement.

Company History

Since 2015 we have grown from a specialist building outfit into a multidisciplinary contractor trusted by government ministries, diplomatic missions, mining houses, and private developers. Our governance framework emphasises financial stewardship, ethical supply chains, and stakeholder transparency.

Philosophy

Our Philosophy is to provide our clients with an assured experience when chosen to execute their projects. Our emphasis on clear communication and follow through procedures ensures that our clients’ objectives are top priority in the planning and execution of all our projects.

Capability Statement

Optimus Building and Civil Contractors limited has an in-house team of skilled human resource as well as network of trade alliance of professional affiliates to manage projects of any size within its portfolio.

Vision

To be a leader in the Zambian construction industry delivering quality service.

Mission

To deliver construction products that are of good quality and within specified time frames.

QHSE Policy

Optimus implements a comprehensive QHSE system based on ISO 9001, ISO 14001, and OHSAS 18001, ensuring regulatory compliance, customer satisfaction, employee safety, continuous improvement, and environmental responsibility through proactive risk management and sustainable operational practices.
