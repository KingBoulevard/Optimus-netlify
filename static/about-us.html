About Optimus

Incorporated under the Companies Act and registered with the National Council for Construction (NCC Grade 5B), Optimus Building & Civil Contractors Ltd combines local ownership with global standards. Clear communication, collaborative planning, and uncompromising safety are the cornerstones of every engagement.

Company Background

Optimus Building and Civil Contractors Limited is a company owned by local Zambians and incorporated in 2015 under the Zambian Companies Act. The main activities of the firm are Building and Civil Construction, Renovations/Refurbishments, Project management and Construction management. The firm also provides technical consultancy and construction services including, but not limited to: Civil works, Structural works, Construction, Water and wastewater design and construction, Electrical, Plumbing, Bricklaying, Paving, Project supervision and Management, and any other general maintenance works.

Philosophy

Our Philosophy is to provide our clients with an assured experience when chosen to execute their projects. Our emphasis on clear communication and follow through procedures ensures that our clients' objectives are top priority in the planning and execution of all our projects.

Capability Statement

Optimus Building and Civil Contractors limited has an in-house team of skilled human resource as well as network of trade alliance of professional affiliates to manage projects of any size within its portfolio.

Vision

To be a leader in the Zambian construction industry delivering quality service.

Mission

To deliver construction products that are of good quality and within specified time frames.

QHSE Policy

Optimus Building and Civil Contractors Ltd is committed to conducting all business activities in full compliance with local laws and regulations. We uphold respect for life, human rights, cultural diversity, and the environment while prioritizing the health and safety of our employees.

Our integrated Quality, Health, Safety, and Environmental (QHSE) Policy ensures sustainable operations, meets customer expectations, and fosters employee growth within a safe and environmentally responsible workplace.

  • Promoting operational excellence and high-quality service, focusing on customer satisfaction and continual improvement.
  • Preventing environmental harm and maintaining environmentally responsible business practices.
  • Providing a safe work environment through proactive hazard and risk management, and preventing occupational injury and illness.
  • Encouraging innovation, knowledge sharing, and initiatives that enhance employee well-being and quality of life.

Our Corporate Management System aligns with international standards: ISO 9001 (Quality Management Systems), ISO 14001 (Environmental Management Systems), and OHSAS 18001 (Occupational Health and Safety Management Systems).
